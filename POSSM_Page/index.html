About

We’ve been told that we have an infectious charm and enthusiasm for life, and we love to spread our energy wherever we go. We’ve been honored to have won Best of Hartford in 2017, 2018 and 2019 and were nominated in 2019 for a New England Music award for best in the state of CT! We are also the proud co-creators of the Hartbeat Music Festival. We love our hometown of Hartford and are thankful for the support, inspiration and friendship given by their community. #HartfordHasIt! Off the stage we’re surfers, teachers, students, business owners and lovers of life.
